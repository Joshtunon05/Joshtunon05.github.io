URLEuropean Parliament – Cybersecurity Threats
AuthorEuropean Parliament
Abstract This article from the European Parliament explains major and emerging cybersecurity threats in a simple and clear way. It describes how cyberattacks have increased and highlights common dangers like ransomware, phishing, and attacks on important infrastructure. It also explains how global conflicts and new technologies have made cybercrime more dangerous. The article gives examples of recent large attacks and shows why countries must improve their security. Overall, it provides an easy overview of why cybersecurity matters and why threats continue to grow each year.
